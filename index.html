About

Alessia

A graphic designer and 3D modeler based in Italy, currently completing an internship at a bijoux and fashion accessories company. My work sits at the crossroads of high-end editorial design and technical 3D precision—from photo retouching for luxury catalogs to building intricate jewelry models in Rhinoceros 8.

I believe in the power of detail. Every curve in a 3D model, every shadow in a retouched image, every typographic choice in a catalog tells a story. My goal is to bridge the tactile world of Italian craftsmanship with the infinite possibilities of digital design.
